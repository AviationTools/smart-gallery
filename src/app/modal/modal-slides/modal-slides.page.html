Quick Guide

Welcome!

Smart Gallery lets you sort your new images by using your created schedule. All of the images and lessons are stored locally!

Add a lesson

Fill out the form, choose a lesson color and press save.

Schedule

Change or delete lessons by pressing & holding it.

Camera

Create & manage your sorted images by entering a folder.

Folder

View images and edit or delete them by pressing & holding the image.

Edit

Change the images folders in order for you to customize your gallery
