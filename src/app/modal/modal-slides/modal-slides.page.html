Welcome!

Smart Gallery lets you sort your new images by using your created schedule. All of the images and lessons are stored locally!

When adding a Lesson

Fill out the form, choose a lesson color and press save.

Customize Schedule

When adding a lesson pick one of the Repeat options to repeat that lesson accordingly.

Starting Week

Pick the week when the lesson should initially Start.

Schedule
